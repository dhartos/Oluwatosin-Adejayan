About Me

I’m Oluwatosin, a user-friendly product designer with years of experience in crafting seamless digital experiences. My design philosophy is centered around simplicity, usability, and accessibility, ensuring that every product I work on solves real-world problems, which is how I coined the word for myself user-friendly and user-conscious designer.

I specialize in translating concepts into functional and user-friendly designs, ensuring accessibility and responsiveness across all devices. From prototyping with Figma and Sketch to implementing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I deliver solutions that enhance user engagement and satisfaction.

I also run DesignCave, where I mentor and support designers in building their skills, portfolios, and confidence. Beyond my projects, I’m an advocate for structured design education. Through initiatives like Saturday Design with Me and my Design Mastery Starter program, I guide aspiring designers in mastering the fundamentals of UX/UI.
My work reflects a commitment to user research, precise design, and seamless development processes. Explore my projects to see how I craft impactful digital experiences.

When I’m not designing or mentoring, I’m curating resources, collaborating on impactful projects, and pushing boundaries in product design. If you’re interested in working together, feel free to reach out!

SmartyHome Control App

A Smart Home Control App: Simplifying the Connected Home Experience

The journey begins with a welcoming splash screen, featuring the tagline: "Welcome to Your Smarter Home." This sets the tone for an experience that promises ease, connectivity, and sophistication.

👀 Problem

Addressing the fragmented and complex smart home control for homeowners who struggle with managing multiple devices, leading to a disjointed and frustrating experience.

♠️ Solution

Creating a unified smart home control app offering compatibility with various devices, user-friendly automation, voice command integration, security, and energy management features.

Key Findings from Market Analysis

Device control solutions such as Google Home and Amazon Alexa highlight the importance of integration and ease of use. Our solution emphasizes these needs.
